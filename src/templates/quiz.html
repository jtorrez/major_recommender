Interpreting Results

This tool is meant to help you explore options. It wont' necessarily suggest things you are already interested in, and for those of you that have already started careers, it may not suggest what you have already chosen to do.

I'm currently working to add automatic feedback about why you received the recommendations you did, but until that functionality is working, here is a quick breakdown:

The quiz captures a macro "field of study" that you might be interested in. There are only five fields of study I used in order to provide diverse recommendations. Your interests are then weighted by how secure you want your future career to be and how important potential earnings might be to you. This produces the final list of recommendations you'll receive.

The more honestly you answer the questions, the better the recommendations will be. But also don't be afraid to take the quiz more than once. Change your answers, see how things change; it is often obvious what the questions are trying to get at so don't be afraid to purposely skew your results to explore new options.
